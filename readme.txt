A plugin which permit to share all your posts on Facebook timeline or page.

== Description ==

Facebook Sharing is able to publish automatically your website posts on your Facebook profile or business page. All of that with a minified interface. All is running naturally !

And the best, due to an approval of my plugin by Facebook Developer Team, you don't need any developer account on Facebook, no need to create a Facebook App to got App ID, App secret key... Just one click on "Connect to Facebook" !

= Features =
*   No need Facebook Developer account and app
*   Connect to Facebook in 1 click
*   Publish text posts on Facebook text post
*   Publish featured picture post on Facebook picture post
*   Publish gallery post on Facebook album
*   For instant or programming posts
*   Choice between your private profile or a business page
*   And more soon...

= Facebook Permissions required =
To publish automatically your posts on Facebook, my plugin needs permissions from your Facebook profile.
When you will connect you on Facebook, these permissions will be ask to you :

*   Receive public profile
*   Receive email address
*   Receive photos
*   Publish for you on your profile
*   Manage your pages and publish on it (if you have it)

We will not use your public profile, email address and photos. We will just publish what you post on your WordPress website to your Facebook profile or page.
This plugin has been approved by Facebook Developer Team to got these permissions.

== Installation ==

= Installation from your Dashboard =
1.  Go to *Plugins* => *Add New*
2.  Search *Facebook Sharing*
3.  Click on *Install Now*
4.  Activate the plugin

= Installation from WordPress.org =
1.  Go to *Plugins*
2.  Search *Facebook Sharing*
3.  Click on item card
4.  Download the plugin zip file
5.  Go to your website Dashboard
6.  Go to *Plugins* => *Add New*
7.  Click on *Upload Plugin*
8.  Upload the plugin zip file
9.  Activate the plugin
